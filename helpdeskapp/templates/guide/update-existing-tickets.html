Update Existing Tickets

Learn how to modify and add information to your support tickets.

Note: You can only update tickets that you created. Admins may also update tickets for clarity and to provide assistance.

Deleting Tickets

Find Your Ticket
  1. Go to your Dashboard - Click "Dashboard" from the menu
  2. Find your ticket - Look through your ticket list
  3. Click on the edit button - This opens the ticket details
  4. Edit ticket as necessary - Ensure the ticket title and description are at least 5 characters long
  5. Click "Update" - Save your changes
When to Update Tickets

Good reasons to update a ticket:

✓ Update When:
  • You have new information about the problem
  • The issue has changed or evolved
  • You found a temporary workaround
  • Error messages have changed
  • You need to clarify previous details
  • You discovered the cause
✗ Don't Update When:
  • Just to ask for updates
  • To complain about response time
  • When ticket is already resolved
  • To change to a different problem entirely
