Creating New Tickets

Learn how to submit effective support tickets that get resolved quickly.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Access the Ticket Creation Form

You can create a new ticket in two ways:

  • From Home: Click the "Create Ticket" button on the home page
  • From Menu: Navigate to "New Ticket" in the navigation bar
Tip: The home page modal is fastest for quick tickets!
Step 2: Write an Effective Title

Good titles are specific and descriptive:

✓ Good Examples:
  • "Can't access email on work phone"
  • "Printer not working in Farringdon office"
  • "Password reset required for john.doe@paconsulting.com account"
  • "Excel crashes when opening large files"
✗ Poor Examples:
  • "Help required urgently"
  • "Computer problem"
  • "Need assistance"
  • "Urgent!!!"
Step 3: Provide Detailed Description

Include these details in your description:

  1. What were you trying to do? - Your intended action
  2. What happened instead? - The actual result
  3. When did this start? - Date and time
  4. Error messages: - Copy exact text if applicable
  5. Steps to reproduce: - How to recreate the problem
  6. Device/software info: - What you're using
Example Description:

"I was trying to print a PDF document from Adobe Reader to the HP printer in the Farringdon office. When I clicked 'Print', I got an error message saying 'Printer not available'. This started happening this morning around 9 AM. Other people in the office are having the same issue. I'm using a Macbook and Adobe Reader version 9.4.2."

Step 4: Submit and Track
  1. Review your ticket before submitting
  2. Click "Add Ticket" to create it
  3. Ensure you have access to your helpdesk account to view updates
  4. Check your dashboard to track your tickets' progress
